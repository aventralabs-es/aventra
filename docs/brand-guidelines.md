# Aventra Brand Guidelines

## Positioning

Aventra builds trading indicators, setup scanners, and platform tools for technical traders.

## Tone

- Clear
- International
- Practical
- Calm
- Technical without being cryptic

## Logo Direction

- Use `Aventra` as the primary brand wordmark.
- Use the selected concept 1 direction for the website and social media work.
- Keep the logo text-only for now: no icon, no monogram, no `Labs`.
- Prefer black or near-black charcoal on light backgrounds.
- Avoid red accents for the current logo direction.
- Keep the wordmark modern, minimal, readable, and slightly technical.
- Treat `Aventra Labs` only as a possible internal or company-context variant, not the main public logo.

## Avoid
- Guaranteed profit language
- Overpromising
- Aggressive sales language
- Overly dense chart visuals
- Long labels that cover price action

## Naming Examples

- `MA Crossover Pullback Signals [Aventra]`
- `FVG & IFVG [Aventra]`
- `Market Sessions [Aventra]`
- `Trend Strength Matrix [Aventra]`
- `Breakout Retest Signals [Aventra]`

## Website Copy Direction

- Lead with tools and use cases.
- Keep product cards concise.
- Explain what each tool detects.
- Use educational language for setups and patterns.
- Keep financial advice disclaimers visible where relevant.
