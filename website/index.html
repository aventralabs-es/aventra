Market Radar

Pattern and setup scanning concept

Market Radar will organize watchlist candidates by technical formations, reversal conditions, and ready-made setup templates. The goal is to surface market context for review, not to provide trade recommendations.

Scan Category What It Looks For Example Setups
Bullish formations Continuation and accumulation structures that can support upside scenarios. Breakout retest, trend pullback, volume breakout, tight consolidation.
Reversal formations Failed moves, exhaustion behavior, liquidity sweeps, and structure shifts after extremes. Double bottom, failed breakdown, liquidity sweep, CHoCH after selloff.
Ready setups Template-based scans inspired by well-known trader methodologies and public education. Minervini-style trend template, Oliver Kell-style momentum setup, Qullamaggie-style breakout scan.
